## What is VibeVoice?

VibeVoice is a **family of open-source frontier voice AI models** from Microsoft that covers both Text-to-Speech (TTS) and Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR). All models share a core innovation: continuous speech tokenizers (Acoustic and Semantic) operating at an ultra-low frame rate of **7.5 Hz**, which efficiently preserves audio fidelity while significantly boosting computational efficiency for long sequences.

VibeVoice employs a [next-token diffusion](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.08635) framework — a Large Language Model understands textual context and dialogue flow, while a diffusion head generates high-fidelity acoustic details.

## Model family

| Model | Size | Capability | Status |
|-------|------|------------|--------|
| [VibeVoice-ASR-7B](https://huggingface.co/microsoft/VibeVoice-ASR) | 7B | Long-form ASR, diarization, timestamping | Available |
| [VibeVoice-TTS-1.5B](https://huggingface.co/microsoft/VibeVoice-1.5B) | 1.5B | Long-form multi-speaker TTS | Code disabled |
| [VibeVoice-Realtime-0.5B](https://huggingface.co/microsoft/VibeVoice-Realtime-0.5B) | 0.5B | Real-time streaming TTS | Available |

## Responsible use

VibeVoice is intended for **research and development purposes only**. High-quality synthetic speech carries real risk of misuse for deepfakes and disinformation. Please review the [responsible use guidelines](/reference/responsible-use) before deploying.

<Warning>
  Do not use VibeVoice in commercial or production applications without further testing and your own safety evaluation.
</Warning>
