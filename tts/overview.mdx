<Warning>
  The VibeVoice-TTS inference code has been **disabled** from this repository due to widespread misuse discovered after release. The model weights remain available on Hugging Face for research purposes, but installation and usage scripts are not provided. See [Responsible Use](/reference/responsible-use) for more context.
</Warning>

**VibeVoice-TTS** is a long-form, multi-speaker text-to-speech model designed to generate expressive conversational audio such as podcasts from text. It synthesizes speech up to **90 minutes** long with up to **4 distinct speakers**, surpassing the typical 1–2 speaker limits of most prior models.

## Capabilities

VibeVoice-TTS is particularly well-suited for:

- **Podcast generation** — multi-turn conversations between up to 4 speakers
- **Audiobook narration** — long-form single-speaker speech with consistent voice
- **Conversational AI** — expressive dialogue synthesis
- **Cross-lingual content** — English/Chinese with accent preservation

<Note>
  VibeVoice-TTS is a **pretrained model** without post-training or benchmark-specific optimizations. This makes it versatile and capable of emergent behaviors (including spontaneous background music and even singing), but also means outputs can be unpredictable.
</Note>

## Limitations

- Code and usage instructions are currently disabled
- English and Chinese only — other languages may produce unexpected outputs
- Does not model overlapping speech segments
- Not intended for commercial use without further testing

For real-time TTS that is actively maintained, see [VibeVoice-Streaming](/tts/streaming-overview).
