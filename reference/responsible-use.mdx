  VibeVoice is intended for **research and development purposes only**. Do not use it in commercial or production applications without further testing and your own safety evaluation.
</Warning>

## Risks

### Deepfakes and disinformation

High-quality synthetic speech can be misused to create convincing fake audio for impersonation, fraud, or the spread of disinformation. The models in the VibeVoice family — particularly the TTS and Streaming TTS variants — are capable of generating highly natural-sounding speech, which amplifies this risk.

When using VibeVoice:

- Verify that the transcripts and source material you provide are accurate and lawful.
- Do not use generated audio to misrepresent, impersonate, or deceive.
- Do not use generated audio to create or amplify disinformation.

### Inherited biases and errors

VibeVoice builds on Qwen2.5-1.5B as its base language model. It inherits any biases, errors, or omissions present in that model and its training data. Outputs may be unexpected, biased, or inaccurate.

---

## Required disclosures

When sharing or publishing AI-generated content produced by VibeVoice, **disclose that the content was generated by AI**. This is both an ethical obligation and, in many jurisdictions, a legal requirement.

## Model-specific limitations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="VibeVoice-ASR — multilingual limitations">
    VibeVoice-ASR supports over 50 languages. However, language support is not uniform:

    - Accuracy varies significantly by language and dialect.
    - Low-resource languages may produce degraded or unreliable transcriptions.
    - Code-switching (mixing languages mid-utterance) is supported but may reduce accuracy.

    Review the [supported language distribution](/asr/overview) before relying on ASR for any specific language in a sensitive application.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="VibeVoice-TTS — code disabled">
    <Warning>
      The VibeVoice-TTS code has been removed from the public repository. On 2025-09-05, Microsoft discovered instances of use inconsistent with the research intent. Responsible use of AI is a core principle at Microsoft, and the code was removed as a result.
    </Warning>

    The model weights remain available on Hugging Face, but the inference code is not distributed in this repository.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="VibeVoice-Realtime-0.5B (Streaming TTS) — known limitations">
    - **English-primary**: The model is trained primarily on English data. Multilingual support is experimental (nine additional languages available for exploration) and should not be relied on for production use.
    - **No short inputs**: Very short input texts (single words, fragments) may produce degraded or empty outputs. The model is designed for sentence-length or longer inputs.
    - **Batch size**: Currently supports only `batch_size == 1`.
    - **Max length**: Generation stops at `config.decoder_config.max_position_embeddings`. Very long texts may be truncated.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Research-only notice

The VibeVoice models are research artifacts. They have not undergone the safety evaluation, red-teaming, or reliability testing required for commercial deployment. Before using VibeVoice in any application that affects real users:

1. Conduct your own safety evaluation appropriate to your use case.
2. Implement content filtering or moderation on inputs and outputs.
3. Test thoroughly for your target language, domain, and user population.
4. Establish monitoring and incident response processes.

<Note>
  See Microsoft's [Responsible AI principles](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/ai/responsible-ai) for broader guidance on AI safety and fairness.
</Note>
